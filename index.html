    The School Church

    Was established in 1978

    The School Church is a unique concept that combines the values and traditions of the Christian faith with educational institutions. It serves as a moral and spiritual anchor, providing students and staff with a place to reflect on the meaning of life and seek guidance during difficult times. The School Church also integrates Christian values into the academic curriculum, promoting empathy, compassion, forgiveness, and ethical behavior. By integrating the spiritual with the academic, the School Church enriches the learning experience and encourages empathy and understanding of different beliefs and traditions.

    What's new

    • Implemented Online Attendance Management System (OAMS)

      Implemented Online Attendance Management System (OAMS): An Online Attendance Management System (OAMS) is a web-based platform designed to streamline the attendance recording process for schools. OAMS allows teachers to easily take and manage attendance records online, eliminating the need for manual attendance registers and reducing the risk of errors or inconsistencies. The benefits of implementing an OAMS are many. First, it saves time and effort for teachers, who no longer have to manually record attendance or manually mark students as absent or late. The system automatically generates attendance reports, which teachers can use to track student attendance over time and identify patterns or concerns. OAMS also enables real-time access to attendance records, allowing school administrators to track student attendance levels in real-time and take action as needed. This can help identify and address issues such as truancy, repeated absenteeism, or attendance trends that could be indicative of broader issues in a student's life. Additionally, OAMS can enable families to access attendance data and receive alerts about attendance issues, providing added transparency and ease of use. This can help improve communication and engagement between schools and families, ultimately improving student performance and attendance. Overall, implementing an OAMS can provide schools with a comprehensive and efficient attendance management solution that can save time, reduce errors, and improve student attendance. By enabling real-time access to attendance records, it can also provide an important tool for identifying and addressing student attendance-related issues early on, helping schools to better support their students.
